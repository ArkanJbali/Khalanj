Our Goals

* Is to protect the environment and it is a charity goal.
* The clothes that shown are sorted and recycled.
* We do not promote or market any particular brand.
* We only offer the donated clothes.

Our Motivation

It comes to help and protect the environment by refactoring/recycling the wasted clothes and support out local community, by providing underprivileged people with affordable good quality clothing.

• We Collect: clothes from bins that allocated all over Palestine. Or we carry out clothes donation campaigns with schools, universities and businesses. 

• We Cure: Collected clothes from bins that were aggregated at our warehouse where it’s graded, sorted and cleaned.

• We Distribute: We set up pop-up second hand shops all over Palestine, where we sell the clothes at the most affordable prices.
